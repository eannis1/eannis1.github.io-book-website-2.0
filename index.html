Biblical Exegesis in an Online World

Using Free Online Tools to Study the Bible's Original Texts

The internet has changed how we interact with Scripture. While we now have constant access to Scripture on our smartphones, tablets, and computers, we also are bombarded by a myriad of internet opinions about what we read. At the same time, the internet has enabled widespread access to language tools that previously were accessible only to Bible scholars with formal Greek or Hebrew language training. We believe this is where the internet provides a unique opportunity for advancing Scripture engagement: it allows all of us (not just those with advanced language training) to study Scripture in its original languages and earliest extant sources.

In this companion website to the course book, we provide additional resources that augment the material in the book. Check here for short videos that walk you through the covered tools and up-to-date screen shots of tool webpages as well as podcast episodes and an online resource list.
